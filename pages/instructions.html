What you need

- A medium - someone who has the ability to contact the internet spirits.
- A group of participants.
- A table. Sit around it.
- Three candles, or a number divisible by three.
- A laptop, tablet, or any kind of electronic device that can give you active, unfettered access to this website.
- One or several questions to ask the spirits.


What to do

- Light the candles and put them in the centre of the table.
- Sit around the table. Place the laptop/device in front of the medium.
- Have everyone place their hands palms-down on the table.
- Keep quiet as the medium recites a welcoming prayer.
- Once a connection with the spirits is made, the medium will ask a question.
- The medium will press the 'conjure' button.
Read what the spirits say and decide for yourself.
